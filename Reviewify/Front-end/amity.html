College Overview

Amity University is one of the leading private universities in India, known for its modern campus, excellent faculty, and diverse academic offerings. With multiple campuses across the country, it provides world-class infrastructure and a strong focus on innovation and research.

Recognized by UGC and accredited by NAAC with 'A+' grade, Amity offers programs in Engineering, Law, Management, Sciences, Arts, and more. The university encourages industry collaboration, international exposure, and holistic student development.
