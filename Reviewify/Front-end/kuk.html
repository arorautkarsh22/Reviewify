College Overview

Kurukshetra University (KUK), located in Kurukshetra, Haryana, is one of the oldest and most respected universities in India. Established in 1956, it offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs across disciplines including Engineering, Arts, Law, Commerce, and Sciences.

Accredited with an 'A+' grade by NAAC, the university is known for its lush green campus, excellent faculty, and focus on academic excellence. KUK promotes research, innovation, and holistic development of students.
